Policy terms

By booking, you agree to pay for services received. Refusal to pay may be reported as theft of services.

Deposit: Online payment is for deposit only. The remaining balance is paid in person after your service.A deposit is required to secure all appointments. The deposit will be applied toward your final service total. All deposits are non-refundable.

Pricing Disclaimer:All prices listed on our website or provided through AI estimates are starting prices only.Final pricing will be determined in-store based on nail length, design complexity, and add-ons.

Cancellation / Reschedule: We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. • Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may result in loss of deposit • No-shows will result in a full loss of deposit • Repeated no-shows may require full prepayment for future bookings

Service Guarantee: If you experience any issues with your service, please contact us within 48 hours. We will gladly assess and provide a correction if necessary.

Late Policy: Please arrive on time for your appointment. • Clients arriving more than 10-15 minutes late may need to reschedule • Late arrivals may result in a shortened service or additional fees

Health & Safety: Please inform us of any allergies, sensitivities, or medical conditions prior to your appointment.We reserve the right to refuse service if necessary for safety reasons.
